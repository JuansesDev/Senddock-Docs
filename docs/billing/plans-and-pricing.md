## Pricing Philosophy

SendDock operates with a **BYO-SMTP** (Bring Your Own SMTP) model, which means:

- ‚úÖ **You use your own SMTP provider** (Hostinger, AWS SES, Resend, etc.)
- ‚úÖ **We don't charge by volume** of sending directly
- ‚úÖ **You control your reputation** for IP and deliverability
- ‚úÖ **No vendor lock-in** - your data is yours

Our plans are based on:
- Number of **projects** you can manage
- Number of **subscribers** you can store
- **Monthly sending limit** included in your plan
- Advanced features (Multi-SMTP, Analytics, etc.)

## What Happens When Reaching the Limit?

### Monthly Sending Limit

When you reach 100% of your monthly included limit:

1. **Sends automatically pause**
2. You receive an email notification
3. You have two options:
   - **Wait for the next cycle** (resets on the 1st of each month)
   - **Purchase an additional package** (see next section)

:::tip Early Notifications
SendDock notifies you when you reach 80% and 90% of your monthly limit, so you can plan ahead.
:::

### Subscriber Limit

You can't add more subscribers when you reach your limit. To continue:

- **Upgrade to a higher plan**, or
- **Purchase an additional subscriber package**

### Project Limit

If you reach your project limit, you need to upgrade your plan to create more.

## Additional Packages (Lifetime)

This is SendDock's unique feature: **Packages that never expire**.

### How Do They Work?

Imagine you have the **Starter Plan ($15/mo)** which includes 40,000 sends/month.

In November, you run a large campaign and:
- Use your 40,000 included sends
- But need to send 60,000 more emails

**Solution:** Buy a package of **+20,000 sends** for $10 (example)

#### Month 1 (November):
```
Included sends:     40,000
Purchased package:  +20,000
------------------------
Total available:     60,000
Used:               60,000
Remaining:               0
```

#### Month 2 (December):
```
Included sends:     40,000 (your monthly plan renewed)
November package:        0 (you consumed it all)
------------------------
Total available:     40,000
```

But if in November you had only used 50,000:

#### Month 2 (December):
```
Included sends:     40,000 (your monthly plan renewed)
November package:   +10,000 (leftover from last month)
------------------------
Total available:     50,000
```

:::info Packages That Don't Expire
Additional packages **never expire**. They are consumed month by month until depleted. If you buy 100,000 extra sends and only use 1,000 this month, the remaining 99,000 stays available forever.
:::

## Billing and Cycles

### Monthly Cycle

- Billed each month on your subscription date
- Example: You subscribe on January 15 ‚Üí next invoice February 15
- Sending limits reset each cycle

### Annual Cycle

- Billed once a year
- **16% discount** (equivalent to 2 months free)
- Sending limits still reset monthly
- Can't change to monthly until the year expires

:::tip Recommendation
If you know you'll use SendDock for more than 6 months, the annual plan always comes out cheaper.
:::

## Plan Changes

### Upgrade (Increase Plan)

You can upgrade your plan at any time:

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Billing**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Select the new plan
4. The difference is prorated for the remaining period

**Example:**
- You're on Starter ($15/mo)
- On day 15 you upgrade to Pro ($49/mo)
- You already paid $15 for the full month
- You're charged: ($49 - $15) √ó (15 days remaining / 30 days) = $17
- Total charged today: $17
- Next full $49 invoice will be next month

### Downgrade (Reduce Plan)

You can reduce your plan, but with considerations:

1. The change applies **at the end of the current period**
2. No refund for remaining time
3. You must be within the limits of the new plan
   - Example: Can't downgrade to Starter (3 projects) if you have 5 active projects

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Billing**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select the lower plan
4. Confirm (will apply when renewing)

:::warning Check Limits
Before downgrading, make sure you're within the new plan's limits. SendDock will alert you if you don't meet the conditions.
:::

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Billing**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Your plan will remain active until the end of the paid period
4. No refunds for early cancellation

**After canceling:**
- Your data remains for 30 days (you can export it)
- You can reactivate anytime within those 30 days
- After 30 days, your account is permanently deleted

:::info Export Your Data
Before canceling, make sure to export all your subscribers and templates. After 30 days, we cannot recover your data.
:::

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try SendDock for free?

Yes, the **Free Plan** is available without requiring a credit card.

### Do additional packages really never expire?

Correct. Once purchased, additional sends or subscribers remain in your account until you consume them, no matter how much time passes.

### What happens to my packages if I downgrade?

You keep all additional packages you've purchased. Only the monthly limit included in your base plan is reduced.

### Can I change from monthly to annual mid-cycle?

Yes. The remaining monthly payment is prorated and the difference is charged for the annual plan.

### Are there discounts for non-profits or education?

Contact us at billing@senddock.dev with documentation of your organization. We offer 30-50% discounts for non-profit and educational organizations.

### What does "Slack Connect" support include?

In the Business plan, we create a shared Slack channel between your team and ours for immediate responses.
